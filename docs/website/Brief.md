## 1. Executive Summary

Horizon Credit Repair (HCR) is a modern credit repair company designed to empower individuals to take control of their financial futures. The public-facing website serves as the primary touchpoint for customer acquisition, education, and trust-building. Inspired by industry leaders like Lexington Law, the HCR website will blend professionalism with approachability, leveraging cutting-edge design and AI-powered personalization to stand out in a competitive market.

### 5.4 Visual Elements

- **Illustrations**: Custom line-art style, teal/gold accents, human figures in positive poses
- **Photography**: Diverse, authentic people (not stock-looking), warm lighting
- **Icons**: Lucide icon set, 24px base size, 1.5px stroke
- **Shadows**: Subtle, layered shadows for depth (sm, md, lg, xl variants)
- **Border Radius**: Soft rounded (8px buttons, 12px cards, 16px modals)
- **Gradients**: Subtle, used sparingly (hero backgrounds, premium features)

### 7.2 SEO Strategy

**Technical SEO:**
- Server-side rendering for all public pages
- Dynamic sitemap generation
- Schema.org structured data (Organization, FAQPage, Article, Review)
- Canonical URLs for all pages
- Mobile-first indexing optimization
- Core Web Vitals targets: LCP < 2.5s, FID < 100ms, CLS < 0.1
- Image optimization with next-gen formats (WebP, AVIF)
- Lazy loading for below-fold content

**Content SEO:**
- Keyword research with Ahrefs/SEMrush
- Topic clusters: Credit Repair, Credit Score, Credit Report, etc.
- Long-form educational content (2000+ words)
- FAQ schema for common questions
- Local SEO for service areas
- Backlink strategy through PR & partnerships

**Target Keywords:**
- Primary: "credit repair services", "fix credit score", "credit repair company"
- Secondary: "how to repair credit", "dispute credit report", "remove negative items"
- Long-tail: "credit repair for first time home buyers", "best credit repair after bankruptcy"
